Frequently Asked Questions about Supermarket Software

The VeriFactu regulations require the software to guarantee the integrity, traceability, and inalterability of billing records. Each ticket must carry a QR code, a recognized electronic signature, and a unique serial number. In addition, the system must prevent the modification or deletion of already issued documents. Salut meets all these requirements natively, without complex configurations.

Salut integrates directly with weighing scales (Dibal, Bizerba, CAS) using standard protocols. When you weigh a product, the scale generates a barcode with weight and price that the POS reads when scanned. Scanners work via USB or Bluetooth without complicated drivers. It is a truly plug & play system.

Our support team completes the import of your items, prices, barcodes, and suppliers in less than 24 hours. We work with the backup from your old program and configure everything before you open the store the next day. The transition is seamless: you don't lose a single sale.

Salut works in offline mode. The checkout remains operational, prints tickets, opens the cash drawer, and controls stock locally. When the connection returns, the system automatically synchronizes all sales, updating inventories and sending tax data to the AEAT. Your customers won't even notice the interruption.
