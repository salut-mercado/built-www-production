Automatic Pricing

Automatic Price Tag Printing

Say goodbye to manual price tags. The moment you register or update a product in the Salut ERP, its barcode and price tag are automatically generated and queued for printing.

Creating labels for hundreds of products manually is a waste of valuable time. Salut automates the entire flow so you can print beautiful, standardized shelf tags instantly.

Whether you run standard prices, promotional pricing, or clearance markdowns, Salut format layouts adapt automatically with accurate barcodes, VAT specifications, and clean layouts.

Sales Impact

Boost Daily Sales by 20% to 30%

Our analytics show that having clear, updated price tags on supermarket shelves directly boosts customer purchase intent.

This effect is particularly strong in the alcohol and beverages section, where adding accurate price tags increases daily sales of those products by 20% to 30%. Never lose a customer due to missing price labels again.
